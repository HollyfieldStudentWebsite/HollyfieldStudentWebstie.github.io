School Map

  • Albury :

    Albury is the oldest building in the school, and it is the large white building you see as soon as you enter throught the side gate. It houses the english department, the hall and the cafeteria. Reception and other administrative rooms such as the head teachers office are also located in Albury.

  • Sharman :

    Sharman is one of the more modern buildings, and is also the largest. It houses science, MFL (Modern foriegn languages) and computing. It is also home to the LRC and pheonix, the learning support division.

  • Oak :

    Oak is a much smaller building, only housing the maths department.

  • Hill :

    Hill is the creative hub of the school, housing all tech departments other than food and housing the art classrooms.

  • Ceaders :

    Ceaders houses the humanities, and is again is one of the smaller buildings with only 4 classrooms.

  • Music :

    The music building is fairly self explanatory, holding the music department. It is the most recently constructed building.

  • Misc :

    This building is attached to the gym and holds the food tech rooms, some drama and some miscellaneous rooms.
